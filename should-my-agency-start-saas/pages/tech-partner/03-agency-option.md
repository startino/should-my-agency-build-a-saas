# What about an agency?

<v-clicks>

- I would never recommend hiring just a developer to build your SaaS
- An agency is better than hiring a developer, but still nowhere near as advantageous as getting a real partner
- **When to Consider:** As a temporary solution while searching for a technical co-founder

</v-clicks>

---

# Agency Downsides

<v-clicks>

- **Higher long-term costs:** Premium rates become expensive for ongoing development
- **Technical dependency:** Limited knowledge transfer creates reliance on the agency
- **Misaligned incentives:** Still not a partner. If they don't have skin in the game through revenue share or equity, the only benefit an agency has over a developer is increased reliability and speed
- **Reduced flexibility:** Pivoting requires contract amendments and additional costs

</v-clicks>

---

# Key Protections

<v-clicks>

- Ensure full code ownership
- Require knowledge transfer plans
- Verify SaaS experience
- Clarify long-term agreements like support and maintenance
- **MUST go through their references to vet them. Call them.**

</v-clicks>
