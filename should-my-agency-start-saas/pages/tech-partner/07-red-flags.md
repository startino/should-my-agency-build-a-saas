# Red Flags to Watch For

<v-clicks>

- Rushing into partnerships or avoiding the "dating" phase
- Misaligned time or financial commitments, vague skills claims, poor communication, and resistance to equity discussions are all signs to proceed with caution
- Watch for previous partnership issues, incompatible decision-making styles, and poor alignment on long-term vision. Talk to their previous relationships
- Blindly "building the vision" without input or validation is not partner material
- No SaaS experience

</v-clicks>
