## 5) Monetization

<v-clicks>

- **Value ladder:** SaaS as low-ticket → education → services (OmniFusion, Pixl8)
- **Separate product:** Sell to other agencies vs your customers  
- **Up-sell:** Add SaaS for new capabilities you don't offer
- **Pricing:** Learn from value delivered, update assumptions

## 6) GTM Engine

<v-clicks>

- Pick one primary channel where your customers are
- Leverage existing skills & assets from agency
- **Mailchimp:** Freemium + viral footer + SMB content
- **Basecamp:** Audience-first (Signal v. Noise) + word-of-mouth
- **Hootsuite:** Free tool → PR → freemium + education
- **Content Snare:** Niche communities + SEO + referrals

</v-clicks>

---

# Mailchimp

- Freemium + viral footer + referral bounty; paired with how-to content for SMBs.
- Self-serve onboarding → huge top-of-funnel; content nurtured upgrades.

---

# Basecamp

- Audience-first via Signal v. Noise + opinionated positioning; zero ads.
- Simple pricing and UX → word-of-mouth inside design/agency circles.


---

# Hootsuite

- Free public tool → PR/tech-press/influencer lift; then freemium upgrades.
- Education (Hootsuite Academy) to mature users into paid/enterprise.

---

# Proposify

    - SEO + webinars/podcasts on proposals/sales ops; integrations (CRM/e-sign) as channels.
    - Started self-serve, added sales-assist to move upmarket.

---

# Content Snare

    - Niche communities (FB groups) + SEO on “collecting client content.”
    - Pre-sold cohort became evangelists; steady referrals within agencies/accounting.

---

# nDash

    - Two-sided marketplace flywheel (writers ↔ brands) + content marketing.
    - Founder-led outreach to seed brands; community growth drives inbound.

---

# EditCloud

    - Enterprise pilots → ROI case studies (productivity/cost) as sales ammo.
    - Relationship-driven sales (leveraging founder and investor networks)

---

# Pixl8 (ReadyMembership/Eventfolio)

    - Deep solution content and thought leadership (presentations, white papers, live demos
    at client events)
