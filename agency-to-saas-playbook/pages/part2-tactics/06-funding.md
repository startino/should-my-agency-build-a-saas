# 7) Funding

<v-clicks>

- External funding: depends how fast & big you want to go
- Bootstrapping preserves equity & control

</v-clicks>

# Bootstrapped Success

<v-clicks>

- **Basecamp:** agency (1999) → product (2003) → bootstrapped profitability
- **Mailchimp:** agency ($300k/yr) → stayed bootstrapped 20 years → $12B exit
- **Content Snare:** pre-sales financed MVP → customer-funded growth
- **nDash:** agency → platform → marketplace → enterprise (no VC)

</v-clicks>

# Funded Examples

<v-clicks>

- **Help Scout:** $435k seed → $6M+ Series A
- **Proposify:** $270k seed → $5M Series A
- **Bynder:** $22M Series A → majority buyout
