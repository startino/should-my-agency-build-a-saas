# TL;DR

<v-clicks>

1. Find problem in your agency (recurring pain)
2. Validate through research & interviews  
3. Build smallest possible MVP
4. Get 10 evangelists from your network
5. Choose complementary monetization
6. Build one primary GTM channel (content/education)
7. Leverage agency cash flow initially
8. Make hard decision (clean break, spin-out, hybrid)

</v-clicks>

---

# Common Failures

<v-clicks>

- No market research (market too small, no UVP)
- Building in isolation (skipping validation)
- Over-engineering MVP (trying to build everything)
- Split focus too early or too long

</v-clicks>
