# The framework to win

<v-clicks>

1. **Problem Mining:** Find recurring pain in your agency operations
2. **Validation:** Desk research + customer interviews to prove market need
3. **MVP Development:** Build smallest possible solution that solves core problem
4. **First 10 Evangelists:** Get paying customers from your existing network
5. **Monetization Strategy:** Choose complementary revenue model (value ladder/separate/upsell)
6. **GTM Engine:** Build one primary acquisition channel leveraging agency assets
7. **Funding Approach:** Use agency cash flow initially, decide on external funding later
8. **Focus Decision:** Choose between clean break, spin-out, or hybrid model

</v-clicks>
