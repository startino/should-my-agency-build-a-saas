# 14. Your 90-Day Plan

<div class="text-left max-w-5xl mx-auto">

<v-clicks>

* **0–30d: Problem Mining + Fit Check gates** - Map workflow pain, score by R×I×F, define go/no-go criteria
* **31–51d: Validation Sprint (offers, LOIs, pricing tests)** - 21-day sprint with concierge trials and objection logging
* **52–93d: 6-week MVP + 2-week pilot to 10 evangelists** - Build focused product, test with ideal users, gather feedback
* **Focus: From chaos to first 10 users** - Systematic path following Basecamp's proven approach
* **Milestone: Hands-on success with evangelist cohort** - Real users validating your solution
