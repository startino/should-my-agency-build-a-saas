# 12. Mistakes & Near-Misses (Useful Tensions)

<div class="text-left max-w-5xl mx-auto">

<v-clicks>

* **Early stack minimalism creates trade-offs**: Power-users churn vs. clarity (Basecamp's intentional choice)
* **Hybrid forever is hard**: Basecamp shows why a clean break simplifies focus
* **Product sprawl tension**: Attention drag across multiple products → refocus needed (2014)
* **Opinionated stance trade-offs**: Proudly opinionated approach → naturally churns some users
* **Distribution concentration**: Heavy reliance on owned media → could benefit from partner/outbound channels

</v-clicks>
