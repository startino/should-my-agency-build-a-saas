## Our Playbook Lens: Monetization Strategy

<v-clicks>

* **Basecamp's pricing advantage**: Usage data from internal adoption, cost understanding from build
* **Their model**: Simple tiers, generous limits, client-friendly billing
* **Your pricing framework**: Value-based anchoring, cost-plus foundation, competitive context
* **Testing approach**: Early access pricing, pilot programs, price sensitivity analysis
* **Launch pricing**: Charge from day one, adjust based on adoption patterns

</v-clicks>
