## The Story: How did they decide to go all in?

<v-clicks>

* **Revenue crossover moment**: Basecamp MRR exceeds agency monthly revenue
* **Team decision**: All-in on product, wind down client services gradually
* **Client transition**: Existing projects completed, no new agency work accepted

## Our Playbook Lens: Focus Decision Framework

<v-clicks>

* **Basecamp's discipline**: Waited for actual revenue crossover, not projected
* **Their transition strategy**: Gradual wind-down, client relationship preservation
* **Your decision gates**: MRR sustainability, team capability, market confidence
* **Transition planning**: 3-6 month phase-out, existing client completion, team realignment
* **Focus benefits**: Resource concentration, message clarity, development velocity
