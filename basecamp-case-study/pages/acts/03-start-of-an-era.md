## Our Playbook Lens: Validation Sprint

<v-clicks>

* **Basecamp's organic validation**: Used internally first, immediate team adoption, client feedback
* **Their advantage**: Real projects = real validation data from day one
* **Your 21-day validation sprint**: Problem interviews → prototype → test with 5-10 target users
* **Validation metrics**: Daily usage, completion rates, user-initiated sharing, retention signals
* **Kill/continue gates**: Weekly check-ins, clear success/failure criteria, pivot thresholds

</v-clicks>
