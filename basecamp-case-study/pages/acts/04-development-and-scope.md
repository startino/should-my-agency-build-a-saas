## The Story: Building the MVP

<v-clicks>

* **6-week timeline**: Tight scope, focused execution, clear deadline pressure
* **Core features only**: Messages, to-dos, files, milestones - nothing more
* **Technical innovation**: DHH builds Ruby on Rails framework during development
* **Design principles**: Simple, clean, client-friendly interface over feature richness
* **Budget discipline**: ~$4.5k development cost, bootstrapped from agency profits

## Our Playbook Lens: Positioning + MVP Scope

<v-clicks>

* **Basecamp's positioning**: "Project collaboration for people who hate project management"
* **Their scope advantage**: Essential features only, avoided feature bloat from day one
* **Your positioning framework**: Against category, not competitors + emotional job-to-be-done
* **MVP scoping**: Core workflow + one delighter, everything else waits for traction
* **Technical debt balance**: Good enough to ship, flexible enough to iterate
