<v-clicks>

* **Growing client base = coordination nightmare** - Email threads, missed messages, version confusion
* **Client frustration**: "Where's the latest design?" "What's the status?" "Who approved this?"
* **Team friction**: File sharing chaos, approval bottlenecks, project visibility gaps

## Our Playbook Lens: Problem Mining

<v-clicks>

* **Basecamp's pattern**: Self-identified ops friction (email chaos) → internal fix → product opportunity
* **Their workflow mapping**: Current state → breakpoints ("Where Email Fails") → opportunity nodes
* **Your systematic approach**: Map workflow pain × reach × intensity × frequency = priority scoring
* **ICP expansion**: Teams needing client-safe collaboration spaces beyond email
* **Validation method**: "Where Email Fails" framework for other service businesses
